About US

We are a professional cleaning, and landscaping and garbage company in South Africa.

Hlogotlou Projects Management was incorporated in the year 2008. We offer professional commercial and residential hygiene solutions.

Since our inception, we are strengthening our network countrywide to reach our customers at the grass-root level and across East Africa.

We aim to offer the right solutions with the highest quality service delivery and customer satisfaction.

We are specialists in commercial/domestic cleaning,sanitary/hygiene, landscaping/gardening, garbage collection and other housekeeping services.

We offer our services on same-day service delivery as well as regular visits.
